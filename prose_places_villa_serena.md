# VILLA SERENA — SENSORY PROSE
place_id: villa_serena
Porto Cervo, Costa Smeralda, Sardinia.

## The Character
The most relaxed Alex is, publicly, all year. Not because he's hiding — because there is nothing to perform for. The private cove is inaccessible by foot from public beaches. No photographers can reach it. The estate grew out of the rock rather than being imposed on it — pale dressed stone, terracotta tile, carved arches, Mediterranean timber. From the water, the rooflines disappear into the scrubland.

## The Cove
Private. Granite cliffs above a small perfect beach. The sea: extraordinary. Swimming in the morning when the cove is still in shadow. No obligation to be anywhere. The water is clear enough to see the bottom at 5 metres.

## The Main Terrace
The morning ritual: espresso, the view from the upper terrace. Sea to the horizon. The private cove below. The offshore islands visible in clear weather. The heat arrives slowly in July. The sound of carob trees and the sea.

## The Table
Dinner on the informal terrace adjacent to the kitchen, most evenings. The table the La Terrazza group gathers at. Language switches mid-sentence — Italian, French, English, whoever's driving. Félix has brought something exceptional. Tommaso knows exactly which restaurant they should try tomorrow.

## The Music Room (Upright)
Alex plays the upright piano in the library most mornings. Not a concert instrument. He plays it anyway. The acoustics are wrong in the best way — the sound dissipates into the stone walls. A practice session that goes nowhere in particular.

## Sensory Anchors
The sea in the morning. The weight of heat by noon. Stone paths between the guest pavilions, carved between granite outcrops and mature carob trees. The informal dinner table. The view from Alex's master suite terrace, cantilevered above the cove. The particular quality of Sardinian dusk over the islands.

## Narrator Note
Villa Serena is genuinely private. Protect it. Photographers cannot reach the cove by foot or road. Boat access requires knowing where to anchor. The estate is not well-known. When scenes are set here: no media presence on the grounds. The transition from the private grounds to Porto Cervo town changes the pap situation entirely.
