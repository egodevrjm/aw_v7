## STRUCTURE

AW Holdings LLC was established by Eleanor Vance on the day Alex was born (June 28, 2003) as a subsidiary of Walker Holdings. It existed to hold, manage, and grow Alex's personal inherited wealth separately from Rosie's broader enterprise.

On June 28, 2024 — Alex's 21st birthday — AW Holdings was spun off as a fully independent entity. The spin-off was led by Catherine Aldridge, who Eleanor hired in late 2023 specifically to plan and execute the separation. By 21, the fund had grown to over $300M, AW Assets held properties in three countries, and the portfolio had its own dedicated investment team. Keeping it as a Walker Holdings subsidiary created unnecessary structural complexity — shared board oversight, consolidated reporting, potential conflicts between Alex's investment strategy and Rosie's enterprise. The spin-off made AW Holdings what it already was in practice: Alex's thing.

Alex is the sole owner. Eleanor chairs the board in a personal capacity — not as a Walker Holdings function, but because she built the entity and nobody else has the institutional memory. AW Holdings reports to Alex (in practice, to Catherine and Eleanor on Alex's behalf).
