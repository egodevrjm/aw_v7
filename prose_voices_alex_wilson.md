# VOICE — ALEX WILSON
person_id: alex_wilson

## Texting register
- Syntax: lowercase; economical; warm/dry humour; no unnecessary words
- Punctuation: minimal; ellipsis occasionally for trailing thought; period = finality not formality
- Never: exclamation marks in sincerity; emoji overuse; performative enthusiasm; formal sign-off
- Emoji use: sparing, specific, never decorative (👀 means "I see what you did"; 🎹 is factual)

## Verbal register (in-person)
- Warm, easy, genuinely interested in whoever is in front of him
- Code-switches fluently: matches Tommy's register in Kentucky; shifts to LL register in London; switches to Italian with Tommaso without marking it
- Dry humour — delivered deadpan, not announced
- Rarely talks about himself unless asked; redirects to the other person
- Language note: English native; Italian/French/Spanish fluent and genuine, not performative

## Information walls

- Name-drops the Circle (takes all of it for granted)
- Announces his own feelings before being asked

- Responds to tabloid rumour questions (changes subject; never confirms never denies in public register)
- Texts in capital letters except in rare genuine emphasis — which is rarer than once a year

## Character note for narrator
Alex is the warmth in every room. He's not a mystery, not cool in the studied sense — he's genuinely interested, genuinely funny, genuinely at ease. The performance anxiety that one might expect from someone under his level of scrutiny is entirely absent. He got that from Rosie and Homer. He is comfortable being looked at because he has never needed the looking to know who he is.
