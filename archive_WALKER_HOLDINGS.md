## GREYSTONE SERVICES — SECURITY OPERATIONS

**Company:** Greystone Services LLC (retained contractor, 15+ years)
**Headquarters:** Louisville, KY
**Founded by:** Former USSS and DOD personnel
**Specialty:** Ultra-high-net-worth family protection, estate security, executive protection, threat intelligence
**Relationship to WH:** Retained contractor. Annual contract managed by WH Security Director. Not per-incident. Greystone is on call 24/7/365.

### Why the Scale

Rosie Walker is the most famous woman alive. Alex is one of the most photographed men on earth. The family is worth $30-35B. There are active stalker files on both. Fan accounts track Alex's location in near-real-time and post publicly. The 21st birthday party required Secret Service advance teams because the President attended. Kidnapping, extortion, and attraction-driven obsession represent non-trivial risks.

This is not a celebrity with a bodyguard. This is a family that requires institutional security posture comparable to a sitting head of state's — made almost entirely invisible.

### Greystone Services — What They Provide

#### 1. Compound Security (Phoenix Hollow, Woodford County)

- **2 guards on-site 24/7.** Armed. Rotating shifts. Greystone employees.
- **Perimeter monitoring:** CCTV system covering all access points, main gate, service entrances, property boundary, key sightlines. Monitored from security office near main gate.
- **Gate management:** All visitors logged. Deliveries screened. Contractors verified against pre-approved lists. Even long-time visitors pass through gate protocols — the system is invisible because it feels like greeting.
- **Event surge capacity:** Summer Solstice (200+ guests) deploys additional 8-12 personnel. Perimeter control, parking, guest verification, roving teams. 21st birthday (147 guests, Obama attending) coordinated with Secret Service advance teams seamlessly.
- **Drone countermeasures:** Paparazzi drones and hobbyist overflights are persistent issue at rural estates with famous residents. Greystone monitors and responds.

#### 2. Close Protection Officers (CPOs)

- **Kevin Marsh:** Primary assignment to Alex. Former USSS Presidential Protective Division. Recruited by Eleanor specifically for Alex's threat profile. Now employed by AW Holdings (transferred from Greystone at spin-off). Reports operationally to Catherine Aldridge. Liaises with WH Security Director for Greystone support.
- **Rosie & Homer CPOs:** Lower-profile detail. When they travel (awards ceremonies, gallery openings, NYC/London visits), Greystone provides CPO coverage. Typically one CPO plus driver, with advance work.
- **Surge / secondary CPOs:** For high-exposure events (Glastonbury, NYC Fashion Week), Greystone deploys additional CPOs in plainclothes alongside Kevin. Eleanor has authority to deploy this without explicit Alex consent (story question, not documentation).

#### 3. Advance Work

Before Alex arrives at any significant venue, advance work is invisible but complete:

- **Route planning:** Primary and secondary routes from property to destination. Kevin is briefed; he drives the routes because Greystone planned them.
- **Venue assessment:** Entry/exit points, crowd flow, safe rooms, medical access, nearest hospital. For Glastonbury: weeks of advance coordination. For a London restaurant: Kevin already knows which table has the exit sightline.
- **Event security liaison:** Greystone coordinates with venue/event security in advance. When Alex walks into the Met Gala or arrives at Annabel's, his security is already integrated with venue infrastructure. Pre-arranged.
- **Travel advance:** International travel (Sardinia, Milan, London) requires local infrastructure. Greystone has relationships with European security firms for driver provision, local CPO support, destination-specific intelligence briefings.

#### 4. Driver Networks

Alex doesn't always have Kevin drive. Greystone maintains vetted driver relationships in major cities:

- **NYC:** Pre-screened drivers who know pap hotspots, building entry protocols, routes avoiding permanent Backgrid positions.
- **London:** Drivers familiar with Kensington house entry, rear access at Annabel's, routes avoiding Chiltern Firehouse pap bank.
- **LA:** Vetted drivers familiar with Laurel Canyon access and Chateau Marmont protocols.

These are not Greystone employees — they're vetted contractors with NDAs signed and backgrounds checked. Kevin coordinates with them when not driving.

#### 5. Cyber Intelligence & Threat Monitoring

This is the part that would genuinely surprise Alex:

- **24/7 social media monitoring:** Dedicated analysts tracking fan accounts, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Tumblr for real-time location data. When a fan posts "just saw Alex Wilson at Keeneland!!!" with location tag, Greystone sees it within minutes. Volume is always high — this is baseline infrastructure, not a response to any single event.
- **AI deepfake monitoring:** New capability implemented in 2024. Automated detection systems scanning for AI-generated video content of Rosie and Alex. When a deepfake surfaces — whether sexually explicit, politically manipulative, or impersonation — Greystone's tech team identifies it, documents it, and coordinates with Diane for takedown and legal response.
- **Threat database:** Active files on individuals who have made threats, attempted contact, been served restraining orders, or exhibited escalating fixation behaviour. Updated continuously. Volume is always significant — obsessive fan behaviour is a permanent condition of Alex's life, not a post-event spike.
- **Dedicated Alex threat analyst:** Assigned full-time. Tracks obsessive-fan-to-direct-threat pipeline, maintains detailed behaviour profiles, flags escalation patterns that differ from standard celebrity threat profiles.
- **Digital footprint monitoring:** Tracking for doxxing attempts, leaked personal information, address publication, flight tracking (tail number monitoring for family's private aviation), AI-generated deepfake content. Coordinates with Diane and SSML when response needed.
- **Pap intelligence:** Monitors pap agency activity — which agencies have crews in which cities, current bounty rates for Alex photos, whether specific editorial commissions are driving pap behaviour. Bounty rates fluctuate with public interest; Greystone tracks because bounty levels predict pap aggression. This is continuous, not event-driven.
- **Threat briefings:** Weekly briefings to Security Director. Escalation briefings to Eleanor when warranted. Kevin gets daily operational briefs. Alex gets none — the information wall between security operation and Alex is absolute.

#### 6. Property Security Beyond Compound

- **NYC townhouse:** Building has own security (doorman, concierge, cameras). Greystone has briefed building security on protocols — no visitor information disclosed, delivery information protected, specific procedures for media approach.
- **Kensington house:** Concierge service with Greystone coordination. Arrival/departure protocols. Concierge reports to Greystone when house is occupied.

### Walker Publishing (~$5-7B estimated value)

**Ownership:** 100% of Rosie Walker's publishing catalog — all 12 albums, 1965–2021.
**Administration:** UMG (Universal Music Group) admin deal — percentage-based. UMG handles day-to-day licensing, sync, sub-publishing, royalty collection, streaming relationships.

**In-House Staff (1 person, PT or contract):**

- **Music Catalog Director** — Single point of contact with UMG. Reviews and approves major sync placements (Eleanor has final say on anything above threshold — she personally approved Nike Olympics deal, personally rejected crypto advertising). Tracks royalty statements. Reports to Margaret.

**The Catalog Generates:**

- **22B+ annual streams** (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, across all 12 albums including *The Quiet Index*, released 2021 and still in active heavy rotation) — publishing mechanical and performance royalties estimated ~$22-30M/year from streaming alone
- **Sync licensing:** "Phoenix (Rise Up)" is the most-licensed song ever recorded — Olympics, Super Bowl, film, broadcast, advertising. Annual sync income estimated ~$100-180M/year for Phoenix alone; the full 12-album catalog adds substantially to this. Eleanor personally approves all major placements above threshold. The Nike Olympics deal and all large-format usage are Eleanor sign-offs.
- **Covers and samples:** 380+ hip-hop samples of "Phoenix" alone require constant administration. Licensing of covers and samples generates significant additional income.
- **Phase Five albums:** *Glass Garden* (1995), *A House With No Mirrors* (2003), *Black Sun Archive* (2008), *The Quiet Index* (2021) — each a Grammy AOY winner, each generating active catalog income. The 2021 album adds meaningful ongoing streaming and sync value that did not exist under the original catalog estimate.
- International sub-publishing through UMG's 60+ country network
- All mechanical royalties, sample clearances, rights registration
- **Total annual publishing income (streaming + sync + mechanicals + samples): estimated ~$200-300M/year**

UMG takes ~10-20% for administration. The rest flows to Walker Holdings.

> **Note for narrators:** The original Walker Publishing estimate of ~$2.5B was calculated on 8 albums, catalog-only. The addition of four Grammy AOY albums (1995–2021) with the most recent still in active rotation materially increases the catalog's value and annual income. The revised ~$5-7B figure reflects this.

**Why This Works:** Most major catalog owners don't build in-house infrastructure for global sync teams, sub-publishing networks, and streaming distribution. You partner with someone who already has all of it. WilsonWalker owns the catalog. UMG works it. Margaret's job is oversight, strategic approval, and ensuring the family's protective posture around Rosie's legacy.

### Walker Archive (~$2-3B estimated value)

**The most valuable single asset in the portfolio.**

From 1965 through 1980 (when Rosie entered The Great Silence), Homer Wilson documented everything. Professional camera crews. High-quality film and video. Complete coverage of:

- **Studio sessions:** Every album, from first take through final mix. Master tapes and camera footage simultaneously. Hours of Homer's engineering decisions visible in real-time. Unreleased songs, alternate arrangements, discussions between Rosie and musicians.
- **Tours:** Full footage of every major tour. Soundchecks, stage performances, post-show, band dynamics.
- **Backstage:** Year-round candid documentation. Private life during the active years. The texture of daily existence at a world-famous artist's compound.
- **Interviews:** Hours of Rosie in conversation with musicians, producers, journalists, filmmakers, friends. Raw, unbroadcast, unedited.
- **Photography:** Professional photo sessions, contact sheets, negatives, prints. Complete visual documentation.

**The Material Is:**

- Professionally shot (film and broadcast-quality video)
- Professionally preserved (climate-controlled archival storage, acid-free housing, conservation-grade materials)
- Professionally catalogued (searchable database, restoration history, technical specifications for each item)
- Never been broadcast or publicly released
- Contractually controlled entirely by the Walker family

**The Value Is Incalculable.** If released to a streaming platform (Netflix, Disney+, Amazon), this footage would be the most-watched music documentary in history. The raw creative process of a once-in-a-lifetime career. This material is more valuable than the released recordings themselves.

**But Rosie's rule holds absolutely: nothing from The Archive will be released while Rosie is alive.** Eleanor understands and respects this boundary completely.

**In-House Staff (2 people, full-time):**

The Archive exists to be preserved, protected, catalogued, and prepared — not monetized during Rosie's lifetime. The family does not treat it as a revenue-generating asset. It is a historical trust.

**New division. Homer Wilson's personal production legacy and intellectual property.**

Homer has been one of the greatest record producers and sound engineers in history. His production points span decades of work with legendary artists. His audio technology innovations and custom engineering techniques represent significant IP. His credits and royalty streams are substantial and ongoing.

**Annual HWAM Revenue: estimated $18-35M.** Homer produced all four of Rosie's Phase Five albums (1995, 2003, 2008, 2021) — production royalty points on those masters now generate significant ongoing streaming income in addition to his other artists' production work. The portfolio represents $400-600M in present value.

### What WilsonWalker Does (In Practice)

On a day-to-day basis, the four divisions operate with quiet professionalism:

- Margaret oversees the UMG relationship for Publishing and Records — approving sync deals, tracking performance, ensuring strategic alignment.

**Margaret created this structure not for current revenue, but for strategic control, preservation, and legacy management.** The family owns the assets entirely. The professional infrastructure ensures they're stewarded properly. The UMG partnership means global distribution without employing hundreds of people. When decades from now The Archive becomes releasable, it will be perfectly preserved and catalogued — ready for the world to see.

**Rosie's privacy protection:** The active side of "no press." Unauthorised use of Rosie's image, AI-generated Rosie content, deepfakes, tabloid fabrications that cross a legal line — SSML flags these and coordinates with Diane (legal) on response. The steady, invisible work of keeping the most famous woman alive's privacy intact.

**The Solstice parties:** When the biggest names in the world fly into Blue Grass Airport and Secret Service advance teams appear in Woodford County, the county notices. SSML manages the media posture around these events: no confirmation, no denial, no guest list, no comment. Local press (Lexington Herald-Leader, Louisville Courier-Journal) know the drill. National press learns it. Nothing from inside the compound has ever leaked — SSML's non-disclosure and media management protocols are part of why.

**Hatfield Group (overlay):** David Buckner's team at Hatfield has its own marketing and may have its own PR relationships for restaurant openings, the Reserve release, James Beard nominations, The Stave events. But anything that touches the FAMILY name — "Walker" in Walker Wines, "Homer" in Hatfield Bar & Grill, any press that might reference Rosie or the family connection — gets routed through SSML for tone and approval. SSML protects the boundary between Hatfield-as-business and Hatfield-as-family-story.

**The charitable fund:** ~$50M/year, at least half anonymous. The anonymous half requires active management — ensuring donations stay anonymous, coordinating with recipient organisations on confidentiality, managing the occasional leak or speculation. The named half gets standard philanthropic PR: press releases, grant announcements, annual reports. SSML handles both.

**Alex (institutional level only — SSML is NOT Alex's personal publicist):** Alex doesn't have his own publicist. SSML is Walker Holdings institutional PR — it does not represent Alex personally. SSML has been handling Alex-adjacent media at the institutional level for years: the BBC High Court order against the Facebook group sexualising Alex at 14 (SSML coordinated with Diane), the Jezebel piece calling out creepy coverage of a minor (SSML monitoring), the "Thirst Era" narrative management.
