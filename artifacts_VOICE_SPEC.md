# ARTIFACT VOICE SPECIFICATIONS
## Tone, format, information ceiling, and update cadence for each in-world artifact

**Purpose:** Before rendering any in-world artifact (Wikipedia, subreddit post, hookup tracker, media coverage), read the relevant spec here. Each artifact has a distinct voice, format, and information ceiling. Mixing them is an error.

## WIKIPEDIA.md

**Voice:** Formal, neutral, encyclopedic. Wikipedia's Manual of Style. Third person throughout. No adjectives that aren't verifiable. "Has been described as" for attributions, not direct claims.

**Information ceiling:** PUBLIC KNOWLEDGE ONLY. Anything on the Wikipedia page is publicly known. Anything not on the page is NOT publicly known — even if it's true. This is the ceiling that all other public-knowledge checks calibrate against.

**What's in:** Full name, birthdate (June 22, 2003), family background (Rosie Walker and Homer Wilson KBE as legal parents / biological paternal grandparents; biological mother Lady Sarah Anne Dawson Wilson 1981–2003, biological father David Wilson 1975–2008), **maternal royal ancestry** (Dawson family / Earl of Fletching / Fletching Park; great-grandmothers HRH Princess Isabella of Savoy-Aosta d. 2005 and HRH Princess Anastasia of Greece and Denmark d. 2001), education (Sayre School K–10; Westminster Sixth Form; Juilliard BM Piano Performance 2021–2025 in contemporary/classical/jazz; RAM junior-year exchange 2023–2024), Juilliard senior recital programme and viral video (Bach / Beethoven / Prokofiev / Liszt), Hatfield Group association, Young Met Board membership, known languages (**English, French, Italian, Spanish**), known instruments (**piano, violin, guitar** — voice not publicly released). **Alex has never given a public interview.** All biographical detail sourced from the Walker Holdings fact sheet (SSML-maintained) and from other people who have spoken about him.

**What's NOT yet in (as of June 2025):** Recording deal or album release. Fashion house campaigns. Modelling contracts. Grammy (as an artist — he has attended the ceremony annually with family since childhood, see Met Gala/etc. attendance pattern). Carnegie Hall or Wigmore Hall recitals. Any commercial activity — none has happened. **Any interview he may give in the future.** He is a pianist who just graduated — no occupation yet, no career started.

**What IS on the record publicly (often missed):** **Met Gala attendance annually since 2013 (age 10)** — 12 consecutive years, Donatella Versace dresses him for most. The 2013 debut photograph is among the most iconic Met Gala images of all time. Annual Grammy Awards walkthroughs with Rosie. Oscars, BAFTAs, Elton John's Oscar Party (godson), Kennedy Center Honors (periodic). Front-row fashion week attendance (Versace, Stella McCartney, select others). Keeneland seasonal. Glastonbury. Young Met Board member since 2024. **Every public appearance until graduation was walkthrough-only** — no red-carpet press lines, no interviews, no quotes. The Rule was strict and it held.

**What's NOT in:** Hookup specifics beyond tabloid-confirmed Ceretti photograph. Private family detail. Business deal financial terms. Alex's personal opinions. AL.X team internal operations. Rosie's ghostwriting credits.

**Update cadence:** Updated after major public events (first commercial announcement, album release, major career events). Each update must be reviewed against information wall — adding to Wikipedia means making something public.

**Format:** Wikipedia-style headers (== Early Life ==, == Music Career ==, == Public Profile ==, == References ==). Infobox at top. Footnote-style references.

## PUBLIC_PERCEPTION.md

**Voice:** Analytical. Third-person observation of public belief. Not Wikipedia neutral — this is a narrator's-eye view of what people think vs. what's true. Explicitly flags the gap between mythology and reality.

**Information ceiling:** Tracks WHAT THE PUBLIC BELIEVES, not what's true. These beliefs may be accurate, inaccurate, or partially accurate. The document notes the accuracy.

**Structure:** By demographic (classical music world, fashion/pop culture, fan accounts, tabloid readers, industry insiders). Each section: what they believe, what's actually true, the gap.

**Update cadence:** Updated after major public events that shift perception. The first commercial announcement — whatever form it takes — will significantly update this document.

**Format:** Prose sections by audience demographic. Not tables.

## WILSON_HOOKUP_TRACKER.md

**Voice:** r/AlexWilson subreddit. Pinned megathread format. Enthusiastic but trying to be rigorous. Community-built. Updates as new information arrives. Includes tier system (Confirmed / Strongly Suspected / Rumor). Fans who take this very seriously.

**Information ceiling:** Tabloid-public only. What a dedicated fan account tracker would know from Daily Mail, Page Six, DeuxMoi. No private information. They have theories but no facts beyond what's been published.

**Format:** Pinned post header. Tier table. Evidence links (fictional but plausible outlet names). Comment thread excerpts. Update log.

**Update cadence:** Updated in-story when new tabloid information drops.
