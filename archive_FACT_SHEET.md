## History of this document

This Fact Sheet was first issued in **August 2019**, prior to Mr. Wilson's arrival at Westminster School, in response to sustained and escalating media interest in his biography. It has been updated and re-circulated annually since. This is the **January 2025 edition** — the seventh issue — and the final edition to be issued under the current restriction.

The practice of maintaining a formal biographical document for Mr. Wilson dates to an internal Walker Holdings decision in 2019. Consistent media interest from a range of outlets across the United Kingdom, the United States, Italy, Greece, and elsewhere made ad hoc fact verification impractical. The Fact Sheet was developed to provide journalists a single, verified, and controllable source of biographical detail.

## The Rule — statement of practice and date of expiration

Walker Holdings, on behalf of Ms. Rosie Walker and Sir Homer Wilson KBE, has since Mr. Wilson's infancy maintained a private restriction — colloquially referred to within the family as "the Rule" — under which Mr. Wilson will not and has not:

- Given public interviews of any kind, whether written, broadcast, or informal;
- Engaged in any publicity activity, commercial or promotional;
- Accepted any paid engagement or commercial work, including performances, endorsements, modelling contracts, appearances, or branded content;
- Participated in any public event or appearance requiring his personal consent as a working professional.

**This restriction has been in effect continuously since Mr. Wilson's birth on 22 June 2003.** It is intended to protect Mr. Wilson's childhood and education from the professional demands that would otherwise attend his family's public profile.

**The Rule expires upon Mr. Wilson's graduation from The Juilliard School, scheduled for 22 May 2025.** At that date, Mr. Wilson will be free to engage or decline any opportunity on his own judgement, without restriction or direction from Walker Holdings.

**Post-graduation media queries should be routed as follows:**

| Query type | Contact |
|---|---|
| Career, professional engagements, performance bookings, commercial partnerships, press requests, interview inquiries | **AL.X** — Mayfair, London. James Latham, Head. |
| Family or general biographical matters, Walker Holdings–related inquiries | **AW Holdings** — Catherine Aldridge, Managing Director. |
| Archival, historical, or family-estate matters | Walker Holdings (unchanged) |

Walker Holdings and SSML Media Relations will not field Mr. Wilson's personal media inquiries after graduation. The transition is clean as of 22 May 2025.

### Interests and collections

Mr. Wilson is a private collector in several categories, maintained over his adolescence and early adulthood:

- **Fine art** — principally late-twentieth-century American work; select European acquisitions.
- **Rare books** — first editions and signed presentation copies, concentrated in modernist literature and music history.
- **Watches** — vintage and contemporary, with a particular interest in mid-century complication pieces.
- **Fashion** — archive pieces from specific designers of the 1990s and 2000s, plus current-season pieces acquired through family and personal relationships with several fashion houses.
- **Vinyl records** — first pressings, acetates, and private-collection recordings. Mr. Wilson maintains his collection both at the family estate in Kentucky and at his residence in New York City.

Collections are not maintained in a professional capacity and are not open to public viewing.
