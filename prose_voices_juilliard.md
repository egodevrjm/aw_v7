## JUILLIARD TEXTURE NOTES

The bond these people share is specific: three years of 8am practicals, jury weeks, Marlowe's Tuesday lessons, Whitfield's theory seminars, the particular terror and confidence of Juilliard preparation. They don't need to explain this to each other. References to it are implicit.

Alex's position among them: first among equals, but they don't relate to him as a famous person. He is Alex from section 4B who played the Prokofiev at the senior recital. They've heard him mess up the Schubert at 10am on a Thursday. The fame is noise to them. The piano is real.
